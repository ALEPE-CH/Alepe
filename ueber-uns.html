Who We Are

Founded in 2024, ALEPE - the Alumni Association of the Portuguese School in Switzerland (EPE) - serves as a central platform for connection, exchange, and community among former students of the EPE in Switzerland. ALEPE aims to foster strong bonds and a supportive network for alumni, encouraging a lasting relationship with the Portuguese school community.
